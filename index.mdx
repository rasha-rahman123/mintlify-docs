---
title: HumanStandard API
description: Verify human-origin music at scale.
---

HumanStandard analyzes audio tracks and returns a structured verdict with confidence tiers, supporting scores, and an audit trail. Integrate it into your intake pipeline, catalog review workflow, or certification process.

  <Card
