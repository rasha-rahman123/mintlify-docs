## What is HumanStandard?

HumanStandard analyzes music tracks using an ensemble of acoustic detection models and returns structured evidence: a verdict, a full set of per-head signals, and a secondary acoustic score that is stable across compression and mastering variation.

Results include **three simultaneous confidence tiers** so you can tune the tradeoff between detection rate and false positives for your specific policy.
